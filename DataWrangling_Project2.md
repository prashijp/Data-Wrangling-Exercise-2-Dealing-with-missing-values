#### Missing age filed with Mean Age

Why would you pick any of those over the mean (or not)?

```r
Original_Age <- filter(embarkedwithS, age != '')

Filled_MeanAge <- embarkedwithS %>%  mutate(age = ifelse(is.na(age), Original_Age_mean,age))
```
